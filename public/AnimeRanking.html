My Top 5 Favorite Animes.

By Edmontt.

Shinometa

Comedy / Ecchi.

It is a great, funny anime that critizes Japanese idiology and culture.

Maryuuinkouga sigo no ttakai

Romance / Slice of life.

Heartwarming movie that shows the story of a girls who's being bullied and how a classmate stands up for her.

Sonobiske doll

RomCom/ Ecchi

Nice story about an introverted guy who's fall in love with a cosplayer classmate.

Bokutachi wa benkyou ga dekinai

RomCom / Harem / Ecchi

Kinda standard love/harem story about a smart guy and three classmates who wants to get better to reach their dreams.

Bakuman

Slice of Life

Awesome history about two mangakas who fight to reach their dreams.
